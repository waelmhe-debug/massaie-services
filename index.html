What We Offer

What We Take Care of For You

From the moment you land to the moment you leave, we handle everything outside your appointments.

Transportation

Private door-to-door rides from MSP or RST airport to Rochester. Daily transportation to your appointments with the same dedicated driver. Not a shared shuttle — just you and your family.

  • MSP Airport to Rochester — Private door-to-door service
  • RST Airport pickup — We meet you at arrivals
  • Daily appointment transport throughout your stay
  • Same driver, available 7 days a week

Accommodation

We help you find and coordinate comfortable housing close to your medical facilities — from private apartments to hotel suites, based on your needs and budget.

  • Proximity to medical facilities
  • Apartments, suites & hotels
  • All budgets accommodated

On-the-Ground Support

We're with you throughout your stay. Grocery runs, local orientation, appointment scheduling assistance, luggage handling, and anything else that comes up.

  • Grocery & errand runs
  • Local orientation & guidance
  • 24/7 personal support
